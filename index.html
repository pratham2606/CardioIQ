Bayesian ECG Diagnosis

A Bayesian neural network pipeline for automated electrocardiogram classification on the PTB-XL dataset. CardioIQ combines multi-scale Bayesian convolutions with a custom Mamba inspired state-space encoder. The model outputs diagnostic probabilities and Monte Carlo uncertainty estimates across 5 PTB-XL diagnostic superclasses.

03 — Model Architecture

BayesianECGMamba

The model fuses three temporal scales via residual fusion, encodes with a Mamba-inspired state-space block (fallback used when mamba-ssm unavailable), applies 4-head attention pooling, and outputs through Bayesian linear layers.

STAGE 01

Multi-Scale Bayesian CNN

Four parallel branches with kernel sizes 5, 9, 17, 31 extract morphology at different temporal scales. Each branch uses BayesianConv1d + BatchNorm + GELU + BayesianResidualBlock.

4 branches · 32 ch each → 128 fused
STAGE 02

Residual Scale Fusion

Three adaptive windows (2.5 s, 5 s, 10 s resampled to 1000 pts) are processed independently, then fused with learned residual connections across scales.

3 scales · energy-centered crop
STAGE 03

Mamba State-Space Encoder

3-layer sequence encoder captures long-range temporal dependencies in the token sequence produced by the CNN backbone.

mamba_layers=3 · hidden=128
STAGE 04

Attention Fusion

4-head self-attention aggregates the encoded token sequence into a fixed-size representation for classification.

heads=4 · dropout=0.20
STAGE 05

Bayesian Classifier Head

Two BayesianLinear layers (128→64→5) with LayerNorm and GELU. Weight uncertainty via Gaussian posterior with softplus-parameterized σ.

BayesianLinear × 2
STAGE 06

Variational Inference

KL divergence against N(0,1) prior is summed across all Bayesian layers and normalized by dataset size. MC dropout at inference yields epistemic uncertainty.

kl_weight=1e-5 · mc_samples=30
